Overview

Africa's leading MarTech —
building for the unserved.

Terragon Group is Africa's leading data and marketing technology company, providing enterprises and SMEs with data-driven marketing solutions, audience targeting, and digital advertising capabilities across mobile and web.

As Product Manager, I owned two distinct product tracks: a white-labeled embedded fintech product for small businesses, and a B2B SaaS enterprise reporting dashboard used by major stakeholders to track revenue and campaign performance.

Products I Built

Two products.
Different problems. Same rigour.

Product 1 — Embedded Fintech

SME Facebook Ads via MTN Airtime

Millions of Nigerian small business owners wanted to run Facebook ads to grow their businesses — but were locked out by the requirement for bank cards or bank accounts they didn't have.

I led the design and launch of a white-labeled embedded fintech product that allowed SMBs to fund Facebook advertising campaigns directly using MTN airtime as payment — a first-of-its-kind solution in the market.

Unlocked digital ads for card-constrained users
Product 2 — B2B SaaS Dashboard

Enterprise Revenue Reporting Dashboard

Terragon's enterprise stakeholders were spending significant time manually pulling and compiling revenue reports — a slow, error-prone process that delayed decision-making.

I designed and delivered a more efficient product reporting dashboard that automated and streamlined revenue report generation for B2B SaaS enterprise clients, giving leadership real-time visibility into performance data.

40% faster report generation
