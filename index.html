About

Hi, I'm Esther — a Product Manager based in Lagos with 4+ years building digital products that solve real problems for real people. My background in Microbiology taught me to think in systems, and I've carried that rigour into every product I've touched.

I've led products across HealthTech (LavenderCare), HR Tech and Civic Tech (WorkNigeria / 3MTT), MarTech (Terragon), and AI-powered productivity (Kiub.ai). I thrive in cross-functional teams, love a messy discovery phase, and always come back to the user.

When I'm not writing PRDs or running user interviews, I'm writing about product management and mentoring aspiring PMs.

Approach

How I work
on every product.

01
Discover & Define

I start with the problem, not the solution. User interviews, market analysis, and stakeholder alignment before a single line of a PRD is written.

02
Shape & Prioritise

I translate messy insights into clear user stories, flows, and a prioritised roadmap that balances user needs with business goals.

03
Ship & Collaborate

I work hand-in-hand with engineers and designers through agile sprints — removing blockers, QA-ing features, and keeping everyone aligned.

04
Measure & Iterate

Launch isn't the end. I track metrics, gather user feedback, and use data to drive the next iteration — always improving.
