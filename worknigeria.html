Overview

Nigeria's most trusted
HR Tech platform.

WorkNigeria is a leading HR technology platform in Nigeria, connecting job seekers, employers, and training providers. The platform spans job listings, professional training and development, consultation scheduling, and skills matching — serving millions of Nigerians at different stages of their careers.

I joined as Product Manager in August 2023 and spent nearly two years owning the product roadmap, leading cross-functional team alignment, and driving significant improvements to the platform's training and development experience.

Key Contributions

What I worked on
and why it mattered.

Stakeholder Management
Structured product calls that drove team efficiency

Managed stakeholder relationships by leading regular product alignment calls — bringing together engineers, designers, business stakeholders, and external partners. This created shared visibility and reduced communication gaps, leading to a noticeably more efficient teamwork structure.

Product Discovery & UI Redesign
Training & Development page — overhauled from scratch

Conducted in-depth product discovery sessions — user interviews, usage analysis, and stakeholder interviews — to identify friction in the existing training and consultation booking experience. Led the redesign of the Training & Development service page, resulting in a significantly more streamlined booking and scheduling flow for both users and training providers.

3MTT Integration
Built the job board for a 3 million learner government programme

Collaborated with a global cross-functional team to build a dedicated job board for the 3 Million Technical Talent (3MTT) programme — a Federal Ministry of Communication initiative. This integration directly contributed to a 7% increase in annual business revenue by expanding WorkNigeria's reach to a massive new user cohort.
