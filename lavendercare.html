Overview

Motherhood doesn't start at birth.
Care shouldn't end there either.

LavenderCare is a maternal health platform designed to support women across their motherhood journey — with a strong focus on postpartum care, where support is most often missing. The platform connects mothers with licensed healthcare providers (obstetricians, psychologists, counsellors) for online consultations, postpartum essentials, and community.

I joined as Product Manager to lead the mobile product experience — defining user flows, collaborating with engineers and designers, running user testing, and driving the app from early builds through to its live release on iOS and Android.

User Research & Bug Discovery

What I found when real
mothers used the app.

Through hands-on user testing sessions, I identified and documented critical UX issues and feature gaps. Below are representative findings that shaped our sprint priorities:

  • Bug
    Cannot attach documents during booking. Mothers were unable to attach relevant health documents when booking a session, leaving providers without context. Logged and prioritised for immediate fix.
  • UX
    Rate filter on provider profile is not intuitive. The filter for session duration/rates required a non-obvious click interaction after selecting a time slot. New mothers were confused. Recommended clearer visual affordance.
  • Bug
    Chat history cannot be scrolled to the top. In the in-app chat with providers, mothers could not scroll back to see earlier messages. Critical for session continuity.
  • UX
    Bottom navigation bar lacks labels. Icons without text labels confused first-time users. Proposed adding labels: Home, Appointments, Store, Community, Profile.
  • Feature
    Booking reason note not visible in appointment summary. Mothers wrote a reason for seeing the provider but couldn't see it in the service summary screen post-booking. Added to roadmap.
  • Bug
    No back button on certain screens. Users were trapped on screens with no navigation back. Flagged as high-severity navigation bug.
  • UX
    End-of-screening CTA copy needs improving. The final screening screen showed a generic "Save results" button. Rewrote to: "Well done Mama! You did great! Submit your screening and a LavenderCare care coordinator will review your results and speak with you shortly."
  • Bug
    Provider marks session complete but app prompts "Mark as completed" incorrectly. Confusing post-session state. Documented with screenshots and steps to reproduce for engineering.
